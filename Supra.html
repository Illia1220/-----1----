Toyota Supra

The Supra has earned a strong following over the years, especially for its role in the Fast & Furious franchise, where the A80 model (built in the 1990s) became an iconic representation of Japanese performance. The modern Supra continues that legacy, offering an exciting driving experience while maintaining the brand’s reputation for reliability and quality. The Toyota Supra is a legendary sports car that has been part of Toyota's lineup since 1978. The latest model, the A90 Supra, was reintroduced in 2019 after a long hiatus.
